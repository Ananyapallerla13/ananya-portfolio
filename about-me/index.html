About Me

Heya! I’m Ananya Pallerla, a UI/UX designer with a background in architecture. My work is driven by a deep interest in how people think, feel, and move through the world. Design for me, is about understanding the different ways people process information, the emotions they bring into an experience, and the constraints they live with. That awareness shapes how I make decisions.

Design Roots

My architecture background plays a huge role in how I approach UI/UX. We were trained to understand people’s needs, emotions, limitations, and behaviours before drawing even a single line and that mindset naturally carried into my digital work. It taught me to design for clarity, comfort, and different kinds of users, especially those who might interact with a product in ways we don’t always expect. (presentation skills are just an added bonus 😉)
